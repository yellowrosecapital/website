# Yellow Rose Capital Website

## Stage 1: Scope, Compliance, and Content Foundation

### Objective
Create a decision-complete foundation for the Yellow Rose Capital website before any production UI is built. This stage locks the public brand position, the page structure, compliance guardrails, and the content rules that will guide the GitHub and Vercel build.

### What This Stage Is
- A locked strategic brief for the website
- A public messaging and compliance reference
- A sitemap and page-by-page content blueprint
- An asset inventory for the build stage
- A handoff artifact for implementation in GitHub and deployment on Vercel

### What This Stage Is Not
- It is not the production website
- It is not a solicitation page
- It is not a full design system
- It is not a substitute for legal review of offering materials

## Brand Hierarchy
- Public-facing brand: Yellow Rose Capital
- Secondary/internal context only: San Antonio Note Fund
- The website should read as a private real estate credit manager, not as a standalone fund marketing page
- The brand should appeal to accredited individual investors, high-net-worth individuals, professionals, business owners, and experienced investors seeking passive real estate exposure

## Public Positioning
The website should present Yellow Rose Capital as a boutique private investment firm focused on passive real estate investing backed by real assets. The public story should emphasize:

- passive real estate investing
- disciplined underwriting
- real estate-backed collateral
- income-oriented investing
- diversification outside public markets
- operator experience
- Texas and San Antonio market familiarity
- capital preservation mindset

## Compliance Guardrails
This website should follow a 506(b)-style tone and structure:

- informational, not promotional
- relationship-based, not transactional
- no aggressive solicitation language
- no guarantee language
- no public return hype
- no retail-ad funnel tone
- detailed offering materials remain private

### Do Not Say
- "Invest now"
- "Guaranteed returns"
- "Safest investment"
- "Best-in-class"
- "Limited-time opportunity"
- "Easy money"
- any public claim that implies a general solicitation

### Required Legal Tone
The public site should clearly state that:

- any opportunities referenced are available only to qualified investors through confidential offering materials
- investments in private real estate funds involve risk
- investments are illiquid
- investors should review official documents and consult their own legal, tax, and financial advisors

## Sitemap
The website will include 8 public pages:

1. Home
2. About
3. Strategy
4. Passive Real Estate Investing
5. Investor FAQ
6. Leadership
7. Investor Process
8. Contact

## Page Intent Summary
- Home: Quickly explain who Yellow Rose Capital is, what it does, who it serves, and how to start
- About: Establish the firm's philosophy, purpose, and investor audience
- Strategy: Explain the private real estate credit strategy at a high level
- Passive Real Estate Investing: Educate investors on the concept of passive real estate exposure
- Investor FAQ: Answer common questions in a calm, credible, non-promotional way
- Leadership: Present Nick Disney and Jacob Martinez as experienced operators
- Investor Process: Explain the relationship-based qualification and review flow
- Contact: Provide a simple inquiry path and the required disclaimer language

## Content Foundation
The site copy should consistently reflect the following themes:

- private real estate credit
- residential mortgage notes
- first-position liens
- Texas residential real estate
- San Antonio and surrounding markets
- short-term rehab / bridge lending
- long-term owner-occupied mortgage note acquisition
- accredited investor audience
- passive participation
- professional management

## Asset Inventory
Use these assets in the build stage:

- logo files from the brand mark
- founder/team photo with Nick in the blue jacket
- founder/team photo with Jacob in the grey jacket
- approved disclaimer language
- approved firm description copy

### Asset Notes
- The logo should drive the visual palette and brand feeling
- The team photo should be used prominently on the homepage and leadership page
- If additional photography is needed later, it should follow the same polished, credible, warm-but-professional look

## Technical Starting Point
For the build stage, use GitHub as the source of truth and Vercel for preview and production deployment.

- treat the current JSX file as a reference draft only
- build a production-ready app structure before styling polish
- keep the codebase easy to extend page by page
- use preview deployments for review before merging to production

## Implementation Handoff Criteria
Stage 1 is complete when:

- the brand hierarchy is locked
- the 8-page sitemap is approved
- compliance guardrails are documented
- page messaging is mapped
- required assets are inventoried
- the build stage can start without unresolved positioning questions
