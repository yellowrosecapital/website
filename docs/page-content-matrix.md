# Page Content Matrix

## Home
- Purpose: establish the brand, audience, and core investment thesis quickly
- Primary message: passive real estate investing backed by real assets
- Primary CTA: request more information
- Required sections: hero, what we do, why investors choose this strategy, how passive investing works, strategy snapshot, leadership/trust, contact CTA
- Do not say: public solicitation language, return hype, guarantees, or "invest now"

## About
- Purpose: explain who Yellow Rose Capital is and why the firm exists
- Primary message: a serious private investment brand built for accredited individual investors seeking passive real estate exposure
- Primary CTA: learn more or request information
- Required sections: firm overview, philosophy, investor audience, what makes the brand different, relationship-based tone
- Do not say: retail marketing language or language that makes the firm look like a mass-market funnel

## Strategy
- Purpose: explain the private real estate credit strategy at a high level
- Primary message: the firm focuses on residential real estate credit, mortgage notes, and first-position collateral in Texas markets
- Primary CTA: understand the strategy or request information
- Required sections: strategy overview, asset class explanation, Texas focus, underwriting discipline, collateral protection, short-term and long-term strategy mix
- Do not say: PPM-level detail, public performance claims, or overly technical securities language

## Passive Real Estate Investing
- Purpose: educate the target investor on the concept of passive real estate exposure
- Primary message: investors can participate in real estate without managing properties directly
- Primary CTA: explore how passive investing works
- Required sections: what passive investing means, why investors choose it, how professional management works, why collateral matters, how it can fit into diversification
- Do not say: language that implies real estate investing is simple, guaranteed, or risk-free

## Investor FAQ
- Purpose: answer common questions calmly and clearly
- Primary message: the process is structured, professional, and designed for qualified investors
- Primary CTA: contact the team or request more information
- Required sections: who it is suitable for, what passive means, process overview, who manages what, how collateral fits in, how to get started
- Do not say: sales copy, urgency-driven CTA language, or promises about outcomes

## Leadership
- Purpose: build trust through operator credibility
- Primary message: Nick Disney and Jacob Martinez bring practical real estate and investor experience
- Primary CTA: meet the leadership team or start a conversation
- Required sections: bios, operating experience, investor-minded perspective, role differentiation, supporting photography
- Do not say: inflated credentials, vague superlatives, or institutional positioning that does not match the target audience

## Investor Process
- Purpose: make the relationship-based qualification process feel structured and professional
- Primary message: investors move through inquiry, qualification, private review, subscription, and ongoing communication
- Primary CTA: begin the inquiry process
- Required sections: step-by-step flow, qualification criteria, access to private materials, due diligence, subscription, investor communication
- Do not say: one-click investment language or anything that makes the process feel like a public retail signup flow

## Contact
- Purpose: provide a simple, compliant inquiry path
- Primary message: qualified investors can request information through a direct conversation
- Primary CTA: submit the form
- Required sections: short intro, contact form, contact details, disclaimer language, risk and illiquidity statement
- Do not say: "invest now" or other public solicitation language
