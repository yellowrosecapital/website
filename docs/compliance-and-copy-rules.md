# Compliance and Copy Rules

## Tone Rules
- informational
- calm
- confident
- straightforward
- investor-friendly
- relationship-based
- conservative
- non-salesy

## Preferred Language
- passive real estate investing
- private real estate credit
- asset-backed investments
- residential real estate lending
- mortgage note investing
- accredited individual investors
- diversification outside public markets
- disciplined underwriting
- capital preservation mindset
- professional management

## Avoid Language
- guaranteed returns
- safest investment
- best-in-class
- limited-time opportunity
- easy money
- invest now
- act fast
- public solicitation language
- retail funnel language

## Public-Facing Messaging Rules
- Lead with Yellow Rose Capital
- Keep San Antonio Note Fund out of the front-end brand story
- Describe strategies at a high level, not as offering documentation
- Keep detailed offering materials private
- Avoid exact return claims on the public site

## Required Contact Disclaimer
Use the following language on the Contact page and in the footer area:

"This website is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any securities. Any investment opportunities referenced on this site are available only to qualified investors through confidential offering documents including a Private Placement Memorandum. Past performance does not guarantee future results."

## Additional Risk Language
Also include a concise statement that:

- investments in private real estate funds involve risk
- private real estate investments are illiquid
- investors should review offering documents carefully
- investors should consult their own legal, tax, and financial advisors
